What is Petch?

PETCH is a health education app that helps young adults build basic health literacy — turning informed health choices into everyday habits for long-term wellbeing. Through gamification and a management → learning → action flow, it creates a personalised and easy way to learn about your own health. A pet health companion — your mini supporter — learns with you, encourages you, and celebrates your progress, turning small actions into meaningful, lasting change.

🏆 Objectives of Petch

Gamification with current topics that spark young adults' interest — made simple yet informative.

🌸 Design Philosophy

DIGITAL
OMOTENASHI

Digital Omotenashi is a Japanese hospitality philosophy — it may sound alien, but it's the foundation of how Petch treats every user. Built on three core principles:

🎯

Personalisation

Tailoring the user experience to the individual — creating health education and actions specific to each user's own data.

🔮

Anticipatory

Petch has the ability to foresee users' patterns through multiple iterations and provide the best health suggestions and notifications.

💚

Empathy

Thoughtful, sensitive language so learning about health feels natural — not an afterthought — making daily actions easier to follow.
